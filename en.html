How to get there

Closest Airports

The closest airports are Lyon Saint Exupéry or Marseille. Geneva is a little further but possibly cheaper.

By Train

From the airport, you can take a train to Montélimar or Valence.

  • Book on oui.sncf
  • Book on raileurope

By Car

From Montélimar, you can either rent a car, or we will be able to arrange transportation to the wedding location.

More information
If interested, please reserve directly with the Domaine de Sévenier and tell them you are coming to our wedding. There is normally a 20€ booking fee. The domain is waiving this fee for our wedding guests, don't forget to mention it when booking!

  • By phone: +33 4 75 88 29 44
  • By email: contact@domaine-sevenier.fr
  • Online: it is possible to book a cabin online on their website. However, we recommend booking by phone or email so you can ask for a cabin close to the reception building.
