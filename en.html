How to get there

Closest Airports

The closest airports are Lyon Saint Exupéry or Marseille. Geneva is a little further but possibly cheaper.

By Train

From the airport, you can take a train to Montélimar or Valence.

  • Book on oui.sncf
  • Book on raileurope

By Car

From Montélimar, you can either rent a car, or we will be able to arrange transportation to the wedding location.

Campings and Hotels

Domaine Les Ranchisses

The easiest is to stay at Domaine Les Ranchisses where the wedding takes place. Hotel rooms are available for wedding guests for approximately 65 euros per person. To make a reservation, please contact the wedding venue directly by phone or email. More information about the venue can be found on their website https://www.lesranchisses.fr.

Hotels and campgrounds near Domaine Les Ranchisses

Ardèche is a touristic location during the summer, so you will find plenty of hotels and campgrounds nearby.
